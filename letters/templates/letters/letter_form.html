Tips for getting your letter published

In contrast to comments, letters are only published if approved by our editors. Views expressed in letters are not necessarily endorsed by GroundUp, but they must meet our quality requirements. Letters are given more prominence than comments on the website.

  1. Keep it short: 100 to 350 words.
  2. Provide relevant information or arguments.
  3. Be polite and respectful. No gratuitous insults.
  4. Do not rant. That's what comments are for.
  5. Use your real name(s) unless there are exceptional circumstances.
  6. Use plain English.
